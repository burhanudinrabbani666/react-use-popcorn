## Components Category

Most of compoenents will naturally fall in one three categories

### Stateless/ Presentational Componenets

- No State
- Can receive props and simply present received data or other content
- Usually small and reusable

### Stateful Components

- Have State
- Can still reuseable

### Structural Components

- "pages", "layouts" or "screen" of the app
- Results of composition
- Can be huge and non reusable (but dont have to)
